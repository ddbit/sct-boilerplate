The BNB Token

The Token will be a ERC20 compliant Ethereum token named "MyHouseBnB" and with code 'BNB'.

The Token sale will start ....

The circulating supply will be capped at 2,000 token

Each token will cost approx. 25€ or 0.1 ether at the current rate (it might be changed just before the launch to get an adjusted rate)

What rights come with tokens? A calendar will be published soon and split into three main parts: off peak, half peak, peak time. The token can be redeemed as follows

5-day booking per person in off peak time = 5 BNB

5-day booking per person in half peak time = 7,5 BNB

5-day booking per person in peak time = 10 BNB

No reedem is allowed for bookings shorter than 5 days. 5 days is the minimum booking allowed (buying at least 5 BNB is strongly suggested)
