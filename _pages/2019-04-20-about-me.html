Davide Carboni, PhD is a consultant and a former researcher now building innovation in sectors like finance, legal firms and public sector advocating technologies like applied cryptography, blockchain and smart contracts. He is the founder of the blockchain company Uniscrow and was previously appointed as blockchain expert in two swiss crypto exchanges like Smart Valor AG and Monetum. He was senior technologist at CRS4 and lead organizer of the first italian Blockchain and DLT Scientific School, held in June 2018. During 2015-2017 was appointed as research scientist for Intel Labs Europe. He published more than thirty peer reviewed papers and articles in main international conferences and journals and co-inventor of 8 patents.
